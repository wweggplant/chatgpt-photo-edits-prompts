Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use these prompts in ChatGPT?

Most AI image editors (like ChatGPT with DALL-E, Midjourney, or Photoshop Generative Fill) follow a simple process: select or mask the area of the photo you want to change, then paste one of these prompts into the text box and generate. It's a "selection + prompt" workflow.

Is editing photos with ChatGPT really free?

Basic image generation and editing are included with a ChatGPT Plus subscription. There are usage limits, but for casual use, it's generally sufficient. Always check OpenAI's latest policy for the most up-to-date information on usage and costs.

How can I get more natural results?

Three tips: 1. Be specific (e.g., instead of "change hair," try "change hair to a long, wavy blonde ponytail"). 2. Iterate multiple times on the same selection to see different results. 3. For complex changes, break it down into smaller steps (e.g., first change the background, then add an object).

What makes a good ChatGPT photo editing prompt?

A great ChatGPT photo editing prompt is specific, descriptive, and clearly outlines the desired style and action. Instead of "make it look better," a strong prompt would be "Adjust the lighting to a warm, golden hour glow, enhance the saturation of the sky, and sharpen the details of the mountain range." Providing context and constraints leads to more predictable and high-quality results.
